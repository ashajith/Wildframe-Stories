HISTORY OF WILD FRAME STORIES

Wild Frame Stories began with a simple yet powerful dream — to preserve the beauty, mystery, and emotion of the natural world through the art of photography. What originated as a humble hobby fueled by curiosity and a deep love for wildlife soon grew into a dedicated visual journey, capturing the raw soul of forests, animals, birds, and untouched landscapes. Every frame held a heartbeat, every moment carried a story, and every click was an attempt to freeze time in its purest natural form.
